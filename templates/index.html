LoRaFence the IoT Fencing Solution

LoRaFence is an innovative IoT-based virtual fencing solution designed to enhance animal safety and monitoring in off-grid areas and farms. Leveraging LoRa technology, GPS, and advanced edge computing, LoRaFence enables real-time tracking, health monitoring, and boundary enforcement for both wild and domestic animals.

Overview :-

LoRaFence is designed for diverse applications, including wildlife protection farm security and animal health monitoring. Utilizing LoRa technology and GPS, it overcomes the limitations of traditional cellular and satellite systems, offering reliable, long-range, and energy-efficient tracking and monitoring solutions.
